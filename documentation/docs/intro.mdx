# Introduction

VibeCheck is a BeReal-like chatbot that aims to provide an opportunity to connect and develop relationships in a professional work environment,
primarily focusing on the cross-interaction between new and veteran members. 
Daily prompts will be sent at certain times upon adding VibeCheck to your Slack group, containing fun and interesting topics. If a user chooses to, 
they can also create their own prompts to share with everyone in the channel.
